E-Commerce Shipping & Delivery Policy

1. Delivery Regions
We ship to all major cities within the country. International shipping is available to select countries in North America, Europe, and the Middle East. Some remote areas may require extra delivery time or incur a surcharge.

2. Processing Time
Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day. During peak seasons (Black Friday, Christmas), processing may take up to 3 business days.

3. Standard Shipping
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days after the order ships. You will receive a tracking number by email once the package leaves our warehouse. Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear on the carrier website.

4. Express Shipping
Express delivery is available at checkout for an additional fee. Express orders typically arrive within 1–2 business days after shipping. Express shipping is not available for oversized items or certain remote locations.

5. Free Shipping
Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping. Free shipping applies to domestic orders only. Discount codes and gift cards do not count toward the free shipping threshold unless stated otherwise.

6. Order Tracking
Track your package using the link in your shipping confirmation email or by logging into your account and opening the order details page. If tracking has not updated for more than 48 hours, contact Customer Support.

7. Delivery Attempts
The carrier will make up to two delivery attempts. If delivery fails, the package may be held at a local pickup point or returned to our warehouse. Additional shipping fees may apply for re-delivery.

8. Damaged Packages
If your package arrives visibly damaged, take photos before opening and contact us within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement or refund depending on the situation.

9. Shipping Restrictions
We cannot ship to P.O. boxes for oversized items. Hazardous materials, perishable goods, and certain electronics may have shipping restrictions based on destination.
