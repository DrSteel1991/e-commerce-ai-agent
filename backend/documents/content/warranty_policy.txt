E-Commerce Product Warranty Policy

1. Standard Manufacturer Warranty
Most electronics and appliances include a manufacturer warranty ranging from 6 months to 2 years depending on the product category. Warranty details are listed on each product page under Specifications.

2. Store Warranty
We provide an additional 30-day satisfaction guarantee on eligible items. If you are unhappy with a product for any reason within 30 days of delivery, contact support for a return or exchange subject to our return policy.

3. What Is Covered
Warranties typically cover manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. Coverage includes repair, replacement, or refund at our or the manufacturer's discretion.

4. What Is Not Covered
Warranties do not cover accidental damage, water damage, unauthorized repairs, normal wear and tear, cosmetic damage, or damage caused by misuse. Lost or stolen items are not covered.

5. How to Claim a Warranty
Contact support@ecommerce-ai-demo.com with your order number, product name, a description of the issue, and photos or video if applicable. We will guide you through the manufacturer or in-house warranty process.

6. Warranty Processing Time
Warranty claims are reviewed within 3–5 business days. Repairs may take 2–4 weeks depending on parts availability. Replacement units are shipped once the claim is approved.

7. Extended Warranty
Optional extended warranty plans are available at checkout for select electronics. Extended plans add 1–2 years of coverage beyond the standard warranty.

8. Proof of Purchase
Keep your order confirmation email or invoice. Warranty claims require proof of purchase from our store or an authorized reseller.

9. International Warranty
International customers should contact us first. Some manufacturers provide regional warranty service; others require return to our warehouse for assessment.
